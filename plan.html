Goals

Goal of this website is to produce an increase of 50% more customers to join the club (mostly younger audience), increase revenues and maximize profitability. Website gives customer a clear understanding what they look for and how to join. Call for action is provided in Home Page. Our mission is to give quality and affordable training to our students in a friendly environment. 

Success Evaluation

Comparing the number of members before and after (website is published) at the club. If we can see more member at the location we can see website has achieved its goal. Site’s success can be evaluated using this process.

Target Audience

  1. Volleyball is a great family sport so we get players of all ages but mostly 35-50. But goal of this website is to attract younger audience and kids to join the club while they’re in high school, so age 15 to 30.
  2. We can gather younger audience by having more competitive games rather non-competitive. Also we can add bars and events to get customers socialize. Having competitive photo galleries to motivate the viewers and a colourful layout with clear texts to grab their attention.
